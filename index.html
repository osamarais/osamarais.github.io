Recent Work

FEqa: Finite element computations on quantum annealers

We explored the application of quantum annealers for speeding up finite element computations. Several key develpoments were made:

  • Exponential improvement in cosine measures
  • New iterative algorithm for search with exponential sampling points
  • Solution of various FE problems with various BCs for 100s of unknowns
